In this interview made at the Play Austria Event 2017/09 in Vienna, Indy Game developer James PATTON speaks with Horst JENSabout his "evil global company simulator" game "Spinnortality". Besides explaining how he self-teached himself programming, James points out how is background in literature helps him develop better, "richer" games than a tech-background would do.
